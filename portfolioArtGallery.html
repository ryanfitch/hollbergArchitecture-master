ART GALLERY

THE FREIGHT + VOLUME GALLERY IS A MODEST SPACE IN CHELSEA, MANHATTAN WHICH WAS DESIGNED AS AN ANTIDOTE TO THE HYPER, MARKET-DRIVEN SYSTEMS CURRENTLY DRIVING MUCH OF NEW YORK CITY AND THE ART WORLD AT LARGE. THE GALLERY WAS ENVISIONED AS A PLACE TO PAUSE AND REMEMBER THAT ART NEEDS TO BE VIEWED, CONTEMPLATED, REFLECTED UPON, EXPERIENCED, AND ENJOYED. THE MINIMALIST STYLE AND MOVEABLE PARTITIONS ALLOW THE SPACE TO SHOW A WIDE RANGE OF COLLECTIONS OF VARIOUS MEDIUMS AND SCALES WHILE NOT DETRACTING FROM THE ART. IN ADDITION TO THE EXHIBITION SPACE THE GALLERY REQUIRED A RECEPTION AREA, OFFICE SPACE, REST ROOM AND CLIMATIZED STORAGE.

WITH ROGERS MARVEL ARCHITECTS
